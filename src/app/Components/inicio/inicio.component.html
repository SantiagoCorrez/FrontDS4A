Our purpose

Flood Early Warning Systems -FEWS, are traditionally based on hydrologic and hydraulic models that rely on expensive and extensive fieldwork and on the heterogeneous engineering expertise among modelers, additionally, their calibration depends on a questionable hand tweak of aggregated parameters subjected to personal judgment and uncertainties from model parameters and model structure forcing the available data that overwhelmingly aren’t able to capture the spatial and temporal interactions of a complex hydrological system and therefore are limited on scalability and persistency over time
